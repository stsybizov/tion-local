# Tion Local

Local **Bluetooth (BLE)** control of a **Tion 4S** breezer in Home Assistant — no cloud, no Tion account.

- Climate, fan, speed/air-mode selects, sound & backlight switches
- Heater target temperature, energy / power / airflow sensors, firmware & filter life
- Native **Turbo** (5/10/15 min) and on-device **schedule** read/write (12 timers) over BLE
- Problem sensor with decoded error/warning codes

Independent community fork — not affiliated with Tion. See the [README](https://github.com/stsybizov/tion-local) for setup, services and credits.
